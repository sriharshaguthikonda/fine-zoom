Fine Zoom Step – Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Fine Zoom Step does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data we do not collect

  • Personal identifiers, browsing history, page content, or user activity.

Data stored locally

The extension stores your settings (step size and wheel behavior) using Chrome’s storage APIs. These settings may sync with your browser profile, but they are not sent to us.

Permissions

  • tabs: used only to read and set the active tab’s zoom level.
  • storage: used to save your preferences.
  • Host permissions: used only to detect Ctrl/Cmd+Wheel on pages (optional feature). We do not read page content.

Sharing

We do not share data with third parties.
